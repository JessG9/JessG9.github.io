APP DESIGN (MEAL DELIVERY)

App Design (Meal Delivery Kit)

“PreMade Prepped” is a food-based service app inspired by meal kit delivery services like HelloFresh and Freshly. “PreMade Prepped” accommodates the needs of those searching for the perfect meal that may suit their strict eating diets while ensuring they get the right balance of nutrients for a productive day. With 6 different diet plans and pre-made meal kits, users can select from a wide variety of options with the opportunity to customize their meal plan with serving size and gain access to nutrient facts, ingredients and the proper cooking instructions. With everything packaged within the app and ready for users to order, “PreMade Prepped” makes it easier for those looking to have everything right in front of them - ready to cook and enjoy! Not to mention a meal that suits their dietary restrictions for a personalized meal.
