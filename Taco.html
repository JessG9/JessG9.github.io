Packaging Design

Taco Kit

This packaging design layout was created with the purpose of providing ready-to-eat taco meals. Using the visual appeal of bright and colourful mexican art style, this design evokes a kid-friendly feel and is designed for everybody to use easily. The presentation board layout to the left was created to complement the packaging design. The process of prototyping and use of font and color were taken into account to clean and flawless appeal to the presentation layout.
