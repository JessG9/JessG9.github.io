Favourite Moments

This video is a photo montage of some of my favourite portrait photos that I have ever taken during my travels. This was my first try at creating a photo montage/slideshow. Editing was used to create smooth transitions, colour contrast and direction of photos as they appear seemlessly after each other. The finished product is a culmination of clips taken on my Nikon D3500 camera and edited in Adobe Premiere Pro.
