Hi.

I started my passion for graphic design upon entering the CyberArts Program at Don Mills Collegiate Institue. From there, I learned and gained experience in a variety of design including packaging, web, and brochure design. I believe that a well-rounded design not only captures the essence of the content, but also displays a way of thinking and feeling that can be passed to the user as a way of thinking made visual. Developing a interest in HTML and CSS, I wanted to input this same interaction design that I thrived off on my designs. This is why I designed and coded this website using HTML and CSS in Atom to showcase my works.
